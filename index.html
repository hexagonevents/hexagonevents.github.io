HEXAGON Colosseum

Hexagon Colosseum is a 3 day E-Sports sub-event under the MMU Vicennial Anniversary which will be held on a big scale which will also be followed up by a lot of other sub event such as battle of the band ,escape room, etc. This event’s aim is to expose the how E-sports evolved and has introduced a new era of competitive sports. E-sports exposure is going up every year, E-Sports viewed by millions of people, and players are fast becoming superstars.
